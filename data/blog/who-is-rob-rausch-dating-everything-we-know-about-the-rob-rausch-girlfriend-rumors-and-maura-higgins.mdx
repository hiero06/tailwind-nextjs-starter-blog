## Who is Rob Rausch Dating? Everything We Know About the Rob Rausch Girlfriend Rumors and Maura Higgins

Whether he is dating a fellow reality alum or a mysterious woman from the Midwest, the "snake wrangler" is keeping everyone guessing as he savors his newfound victory and considerable winnings.

### The Traitors Season 4: A Victory and a Mystery

Rob Rausch’s journey on The Traitors Season 4 was nothing short of remarkable. Entering Ardross Castle as a day-one Traitor, he played a long, strategic, and often ruthless game. Throughout the season, he navigated the treacherous waters of the Scottish Highlands, eventually backstabbing allies like Lisa Rinna, Candiace Dillard Bassett, and finally, K-pop veteran Eric Nam, to claim the entire $220,800 prize pot.

However, while the game revolved around murder and deception, the audience was often distracted by the undeniable spark between Rob and fellow Love Island alum Maura Higgins. Their close proximity in the castle led to immediate speculation that a romance was blooming amidst the shadows of the turret. As the season progressed, the question of whether Maura Higgins would become Rob Rausch’s girlfriend became one of the most discussed topics among the show’s dedicated fanbase.

### Key Highlights & Standouts

Speculation regarding Rob’s romantic status has centered on three main possibilities: a secret romance with Maura Higgins, a rumored new flame in Kansas City, or a reunion with a past partner. Here are the most significant moments that have fueled the search for Rob Rausch’s girlfriend.

#### The Maura Higgins Romance Rumors: "Just Siblings?"

Maura Higgins and Rob Rausch were arguably one of the most captivating duos in the castle, and their natural chemistry was a highlight for many viewers. Rumors intensified when a video surfaced on social media appearing to show Rob touching Maura’s leg under a table. However, Maura was quick to shut down the romance talk in an exclusive interview with Us Weekly. She claimed she views Rob "literally like a brother" and clarified that he was actually touching the chair, not her leg. Rob echoed this sentiment, stating, "We did not [hook up], and we will not. We are just friends."

#### The $10,000 Birkin Bag Promise

One of the most memorable moments following the finale involved Rob’s attempt to stay in Maura’s "good graces" after his strategic betrayal. To apologize for lying to her face to win the grand prize, Rob promised to buy Maura a luxury Birkin bag. During the reunion, it was revealed that he is enlisting the help of Lisa Rinna, a known connoisseur of high-end fashion, to source the rare accessory. While Maura joked that Rob would "never have a girlfriend" because he is such a good liar, she admitted that if she gets the bag, he is "fully forgiven."

While Maura and Rob maintain they are just friends, internet sleuths have pointed in another direction. Gossip accounts like DeuxMoi and the TikTok account "Bravo Breaking News" have linked Rob to a woman named Chloe from Kansas City. According to these rumors, Chloe is a 21 or 22-year-old esthetician. A photo submitted to DeuxMoi reportedly showed Rob with a "new girl in KC," sparking a wave of searches for "who is Rob Rausch dating" outside of the reality TV bubble.

#### A Reunion with Andrea?

Adding another layer to his complex dating history, reports emerged that Rob recently reunited with his former Love Island partner, Andrea. While sources indicated that Andrea was "surprised" to see him, the encounter was reportedly positive. Despite the friendly reunion, there has been no confirmation that the two have officially rekindled their romance, leaving the title of Rob Rausch’s girlfriend still officially vacant in the eyes of the public.

Interestingly, contestants are strictly forbidden from discussing the game when cameras are off, meaning any genuine "flirting" or connection-building between Rob and Maura had to happen under the watchful eye of the production crew or during the brief car rides to challenges.
