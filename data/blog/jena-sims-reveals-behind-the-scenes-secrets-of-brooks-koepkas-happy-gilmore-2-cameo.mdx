## Jena Sims Reveals Behind-the-Scenes Secrets of Brooks Koepka's 'Happy Gilmore 2' Cameo

Model and actress Jena Sims recently shared insights into her and her husband, pro golfer Brooks Koepka's, experience filming cameos for the highly anticipated Netflix release, 'Happy Gilmore 2'. Sims explained how she guided Koepka through the process, providing a glimpse into the world of on-set acting.

### Event/Topic Overview

Jena Sims, known for her roles in 'Sharknado 5: Global Swarming' and as a Sports Illustrated model, discussed her cameo in 'Happy Gilmore 2' alongside her husband, Brooks Koepka. The movie, which premiered on Netflix on July 25, 2025, features Koepka playing himself alongside other golf stars. Sims revealed that she helped Koepka prepare for his role, guiding him through script reading and understanding the nuances of acting.

### Key Highlights & Standouts

Sims shared that Koepka felt like he was entering her world, as she typically navigates the acting scene while he dominates the golf course. Her assistance was crucial in helping him feel comfortable and prepared for his cameo.

#### Learning the Ropes: Script Reading

Sims took on the role of coach, teaching Koepka how to read a script effectively and identify key elements within it. This was a new experience for the professional golfer, who is more accustomed to reading greens than lines.

#### Stepping Out of the Comfort Zone

Sims emphasized the rarity of seeing Koepka outside of his athletic comfort zone. His willingness to participate in 'Happy Gilmore 2' provided a unique opportunity for him to explore a different creative avenue. The role, while not requiring extensive acting, allowed him to portray himself, adding a layer of authenticity to the film.

### Public & Critical Reaction

The film's release has generated considerable buzz, with fans eager to see the cameos from both Sims and Koepka. The couple's involvement adds to the star-studded cast and enhances the overall appeal of the sequel.

### Behind the Scenes / Background

Sims’ experience in the entertainment industry provided her with the expertise to guide Koepka through his cameo. Her support was instrumental in ensuring he felt confident and ready to participate in the filming process. Koepka is one of many actors appearing with John Daly, Rory McIlroy, and Bryson DeChambeau in the movie.

### Significance & What's Next

'Happy Gilmore 2' marks a fun departure for Brooks Koepka, allowing him to engage with his fans in a new and entertaining way. For Jena Sims, it reinforces her multifaceted career, balancing modeling, acting, and supporting her husband’s ventures. The film’s success is anticipated to further elevate their profiles and open doors for future collaborations.

With 'Happy Gilmore 2' now streaming, fans can enjoy the blend of comedy and sports, along with the special appearances from Jena Sims and Brooks Koepka. The behind-the-scenes insights shared by Sims add an extra layer of appreciation for their contributions to the film.
