## Mexico vs. Türkiye: A Soccer Showdown in Chapel Hill – Everything You Need to Know

### Quick Context

The upcoming match between Mexico and Türkiye is part of MexTour 2025 and promises to bring exciting international soccer action to Kenan Stadium in Chapel Hill. This event marks a significant moment for the town, showcasing its ability to host major international sporting events and attract diverse audiences.

#### Surprising Fact: This contest will mark the Mexican National Team’s first visit to Chapel Hill.

#### Key Detail: Türkiye was a EURO quarterfinalist in 2024.

The game isn't just a sporting event; it's a chance for Chapel Hill to shine on a global stage, boosting local tourism and highlighting the region's vibrant sports culture. Understanding the logistical and economic factors surrounding the game provides crucial insight into its overall impact.

### What You Need to Know

The Mexico vs. Türkiye match is scheduled for June 10, 2025, at Kenan Stadium. Here's a breakdown of the essential information you need to know before heading to the game:

#### Parking Information:

Public parking passes are available for purchase on the Carolina Athletics website. It is crucial to pre-purchase parking, as no parking passes will be sold on campus on match day. Several parking locations are available, including Craige Deck, Rams Head Deck, Ehringhaus Lot, Boshamer Lot, and Navy Field Lot. Accessibility parking is also available at Bell Tower Deck but requires a valid state disability plate, placard, or permit.

#### Public Transportation and Ride-Sharing:

Ride-share services like Uber and Lyft have designated drop-off and pick-up areas along the South Columbia bus lane between Manning Drive and South Road. RV parking is not available in any University parking lot for this match.

#### Employee and Student Parking:

Normal daytime parking operations will be in effect for employees until 5 p.m. Employees reporting to work after 5 p.m. who are not attending the match can park in designated employee game day lots by presenting a valid University or hospital ID. Resident students parking in resident student lots will not need to relocate for the event but must present their UNC One Card at entry.

#### Traffic Information:

Expect heavy traffic from 4 to 8 p.m. and 10 to 11:30 p.m. Plan travel routes in advance and consider using the park and ride option to avoid campus congestion. Some campus roads will be restricted to manage traffic and stadium access, allowing only emergency vehicles, buses, and permit holders.

#### Accessibility:

The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill is committed to providing an inclusive and accessible environment. Accessibility parking is available, and the game day experience is designed to accommodate individuals with disabilities. Always check the Carolina Athletics website for specific accessibility information and resources.
