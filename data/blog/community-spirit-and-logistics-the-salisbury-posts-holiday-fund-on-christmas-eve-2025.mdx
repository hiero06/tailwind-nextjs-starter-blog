#### Core Development: Salisbury Post's Christmas Happiness Fund Nears Goal on Christmas Eve

The fund received a timely boost as four more donations arrived just before the holiday, pushing the total past $30,000. These contributions, made both in person and through the mail, will enable the fund to assist more families. Noteworthy donations included $50 in memory of Joe Louis and Hazel Gaither by Ann Gaither Simmons; two $150 contributions in loving memory of Irvin and Melba Parnell and Dimp and Myrtice Roberts, respectively, both from Ginny P Mabey and Andy Parnell; and a $25 contribution in memory of John and Catherine Kirk, and in honor of their children, by Joseph Kirk.

#### Essential Information: How to Contribute

For those inspired to contribute, the process is straightforward. Donations can be dropped off directly at the Salisbury Post’s office, located at 131 W. Innes Street. Alternatively, contributions can be mailed to the previously noted P.O. Box address. It’s important to note that the Salisbury Post accepts cash and check donations but cannot accept credit or debit card contributions. This detail is critical for potential donors planning their contributions, especially when considering how postal operating hours might affect their ability to send a check.
