#### Shocking Exit on _Chicago Fire_ Stuns Fans

In a surprising twist, the latest season of NBC's _Chicago Fire_ saw a major character make an unexpected departure. During the November 5th episode, Miranda Rae Mayo’s character, Stella Kidd, announced she would be taking a temporary leave of absence from Firehouse 51. This heartfelt decision was made to support her foster child, Isaiah, whose mother is in a coma. Stella's future on the series remains uncertain, causing a significant stir among loyal viewers of the long-running drama.

### Behind the Scenes / Background

The stunning visuals of Prime Video's _Maxton Hall_ are a key aspect of its allure, and the filming locations are as magnificent as they seem onscreen. The Gothic Maxton Hall castle is not CGI but the real Marienburg Castle, a 19th-century neo-Gothic structure near Hanover, Germany. Interestingly, what appears to be a glamorous London luxury boutique in the series is actually the Museum Barberini in Potsdam, Germany. The production skillfully integrated real locations across England and Germany to create a cohesive and authentic world. For Ruby's character, her dreams are rooted in Oxford, with scenes filmed at actual locations like St Hilda's College and the historic Turf Tavern, a legendary pub frequented by students and literary figures for centuries.
