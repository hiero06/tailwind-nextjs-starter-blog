## Macy's 4th of July Fireworks 2025: Start Time, Streaming Guide, and Star-Studded Lineup

Get ready to celebrate Independence Day with a bang! Macy's 4th of July Fireworks spectacular is back, promising a dazzling display of pyrotechnics and musical performances. Here's everything you need to know about when and where to watch the show, both online and on TV.

### Event Overview

The 49th edition of Macy's 4th of July Fireworks will light up the sky on July 4, 2025. Hosted by Ariana DeBose, the event will feature a star-studded lineup of performers, including the Jonas Brothers, Eric Church, Lenny Kravitz, Ava Max, Keke Palmer, and Trisha Yearwood. The fireworks will launch from the Brooklyn Bridge and four barges in the East River, offering a breathtaking spectacle for viewers in New York City and those watching from home.

This year's show is set to feature more than 80,000 shells, showcasing 30 vibrant colors and state-of-the-art projection mapping on the Brooklyn Bridge. Questlove and James Poyser have created a special musical score to accompany the fireworks display, promising a synchronized audio-visual experience.

### Key Highlights & Standouts

#### Musical Performances

The Macy's 4th of July Fireworks show is known for its incredible musical performances, and 2025 is no exception. With a lineup that includes pop sensations like the Jonas Brothers, rock legends like Lenny Kravitz, and country stars like Eric Church and Trisha Yearwood, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Keke Palmer and Ava Max will also bring their unique styles to the stage, making for a diverse and exciting musical program.

#### Pyrotechnic Display

This year's fireworks display promises to be bigger and better than ever. With more than 80,000 shells being launched from the Brooklyn Bridge and surrounding barges, viewers can expect a stunning visual spectacle. The inclusion of state-of-the-art projection mapping on the Brooklyn Bridge will add an extra layer of wow factor to the show.

#### Questlove and James Poyser's Musical Score

The musical score created by Questlove and James Poyser is a highly anticipated element of the show. Their expertise in music production is sure to enhance the emotional impact of the fireworks display, creating a seamless and unforgettable experience for viewers.

### Public & Critical Reaction

The Macy's 4th of July Fireworks are a beloved tradition, and anticipation is high for the 2025 edition. The announcement of the performer lineup and the promise of an enhanced pyrotechnic display have generated significant buzz on social media and in the press. With a mix of popular music and cutting-edge visual effects, the show is expected to draw a large audience both in person and online.

### Significance & What's Next

The Macy's 4th of July Fireworks hold a special place in American culture, symbolizing unity, patriotism, and celebration. As one of the largest and most spectacular fireworks displays in the country, it serves as a focal point for Independence Day festivities. The event not only provides entertainment but also fosters a sense of community and national pride.

Looking ahead, Macy's 4th of July Fireworks will celebrate its 50th anniversary in 2026, promising an even more extravagant celebration. As technology advances, future shows are likely to incorporate even more innovative visual effects and musical collaborations, continuing to evolve the tradition while maintaining its core values.

### How to Watch the Fireworks

Macy's 4th of July Fireworks airs from 8:00-10:00 p.m. ET on NBC and Peacock. A condensed replay of the event airs from 10:00-11:00 p.m. ET on NBC. You can also stream the fireworks live on NBC.com with an active subscription to fuboTV, Sling TV, YouTube TV, Hulu + Live TV, or DirecTV. YouTube TV, fuboTV, and Hulu + Live TV offer free trials for eligible subscribers.

You can also watch _A Capitol Fourth_ on PBS (8:00 p.m. ET), the _Boston Pops Fireworks Spectacular_ on The CW (9:00 p.m. ET), or _The Fourth in America_ on CNN (7:00 p.m. ET).

Don't miss out on the spectacular Macy's 4th of July Fireworks! Tune in to NBC or stream live on Peacock to experience the magic. Happy Independence Day!
