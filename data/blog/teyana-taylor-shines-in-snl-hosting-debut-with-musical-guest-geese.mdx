## Teyana Taylor Shines in SNL Hosting Debut with Musical Guest Geese

Get ready for a night of laughs and incredible performances as "Saturday Night Live" returns with a brand new episode! This week, multi-talented star Teyana Taylor makes her highly anticipated hosting debut, joined by indie rock sensations Geese as the musical guest.

### Event Overview

Season 51 of "Saturday Night Live" continues its exciting run with a live episode airing this Saturday, January 24, 2026. Following "Stranger Things" star Finn Wolfhard's successful hosting stint last week, it's Teyana Taylor's turn to command the iconic SNL stage, bringing her unique energy and style to the renowned comedy institution.

The episode promises a fresh blend of comedy and music, featuring the Golden Globe-winning actress in her inaugural role as host. Joining her for the evening will be the critically acclaimed indie rock band Geese, set to deliver a memorable performance marking their first appearance on the show this season.

Viewers can tune in to catch all the live action from New York at 11:30 p.m. ET / 8:30 PT on NBC. For those who miss the live broadcast, full episodes will be available to stream the very next day on Peacock, ensuring no one misses Taylor's much-anticipated debut.

### Key Highlights & Standouts

#### Teyana Taylor's Triumphant Hosting Debut

This evening marks a significant milestone for Teyana Taylor, as she takes on the role of host for "Saturday Night Live" for the first time. This debut comes hot on the heels of an extraordinary month for the star; she recently secured her first Oscar nomination for her captivating performance in "One Battle After Another," just days after winning a Golden Globe for Best Supporting Actress in a Motion Picture for the same film. Her role as revolutionary Perfidia Beverly Hills has clearly resonated with critics, setting a high bar for her SNL performance.

#### Geese Takes the Stage as Musical Guest

Accompanying Teyana Taylor's hosting duties, the dynamic indie rock band Geese will serve as this week's musical guest. This appearance follows the release of their critically acclaimed fourth studio album, "Getting Killed," in September 2025. Fans are eager to see what the band brings to the SNL stage, showcasing their unique sound to a massive audience.

#### An Iconic Promo Pays Homage to Angela Bassett

In anticipation of her hosting gig, Teyana Taylor wowed fans with a promotional sketch that recreated Angela Bassett's unforgettable "Waiting to Exhale" scene. The parody, featuring SNL cast members Ashley Padilla and Andrew Dismukes, earned a direct and enthusiastic endorsement from Bassett herself, who commented, "I love this sooo sooo much!!!! You got me GOOD! xoAng.” This viral moment only built further excitement for Taylor's SNL appearance.

#### Fashion Forward: Teyana Taylor's Balenciaga Look

Known for her impeccable style, Teyana Taylor made a significant fashion statement for her "Saturday Night Live" hosting duties. She graced the stage in a striking red-and-pink ensemble from Pierpaolo Piccioli’s Balenciaga debut collection. The Spring/Summer 2026 design featured a boxy, cropped leather tee with short sleeves and long burgundy gloves, perfectly complemented by a whimsical low-rise skirt with padded hips and rows of feather accents. Taylor's bold sartorial choice cemented her status as a style icon, adding an extra layer of visual flair to her memorable night.

#### The Dynamic Ensemble of SNL Season 51

The cast for "SNL" Season 51 continues to deliver top-tier comedy. Viewers watching Teyana Taylor this week will see familiar faces like veteran comic Kenan Thompson, along with breakout stars such as Marcello Hernández, Michael Che, Colin Jost, Mikey Day, Chloe Fineman, and James Austin Johnson. The season also welcomed new featured players including Tommy Brennan, Jeremy Culhane, Kam Patterson, and Veronika Slowikowska, promising fresh talent alongside established favorites.

### Public & Critical Reaction

The buzz surrounding Teyana Taylor's "Saturday Night Live" debut has been overwhelmingly positive. Her Angela Bassett parody promo not only delighted fans but also earned high praise from the legendary actress herself, highlighting strong public anticipation for her comedic timing. Even "SNL" cast member Marcello Hernández couldn't help but crack a joke about Teyana Taylor, Geese, and himself being his "dream brunch rotation," showcasing the engaging chemistry viewers can expect from this week's episode.

### Behind the Scenes / Background

This episode marks a new chapter for "Saturday Night Live." It is notably the first episode in over eight seasons since the departure of beloved cast member Bowen Yang, who confirmed his exit from the sketch comedy show at the end of 2025. The show's return on January 17, with Finn Wolfhard and A$AP Rocky, signaled the end of a holiday break and the full swing of Season 51, leading up to the excitement of Teyana Taylor's turn as host.

### Significance & What's Next

Teyana Taylor's role as host is a testament to her versatility and growing stature in the entertainment world, consolidating her position as a major force in Hollywood following her recent critical acclaim. Her presence underscores SNL's commitment to featuring diverse and impactful personalities.

The excitement for Season 51 continues beyond this evening. Looking ahead, actor Alexander Skarsgård is slated to make his hosting debut on January 31, with chart-topping rapper Cardi B returning as the musical guest. This continuous lineup of high-profile hosts and dynamic musical guests ensures that "Saturday Night Live" remains a must-watch event. Be sure to catch all new episodes airing at 11:30 p.m. ET / 8:30 PT on NBC, with streaming available the next day on Peacock.

This week's episode, featuring Teyana Taylor and Geese, is shaping up to be a standout event in "Saturday Night Live" Season 51. From Taylor's impressive acting accolades to her anticipated comedic performance and Geese's indie rock sound, there’s plenty to look forward to.
