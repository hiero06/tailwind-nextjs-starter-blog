## Kerry Washington to Headline Apple TV+'s 2026 Slate with Psychological Thriller 'Imperfect Women'

One of Hollywood's most acclaimed actresses, Kerry Washington, is set to return to the small screen, headlining an extensive 2026 lineup for Apple TV+ that heavily features star-studded original content.

Her starring role in the psychological thriller ‘Imperfect Women’ is poised to be one of the year's most anticipated streaming releases.

### Kerry Washington Stars in 'Imperfect Women'

Kerry Washington has been confirmed to star in the upcoming Apple TV+ psychological thriller series, ‘Imperfect Women,’ set to premiere in spring 2026.

The show, premiering March 18, is based on Araminta Hall’s 2020 novel of the same name and will be an eight-episode series. It promises an intense exploration of profound human themes.

The series centers on the dissolution of a decades-long friendship among three women following a devastating crime. It explores themes of betrayal, guilt, and the complex secrets often concealed within seemingly perfect relationships.

Washington is joined by a formidable ensemble cast, sharing the screen with fellow celebrated actresses Elisabeth Moss and Kate Mara, positioning it as one of the year's most high-profile collaborations announced for the streaming platform.

### Key Highlights & Standouts of the 2026 Apple TV+ Slate

The addition of a powerhouse performer like Kerry Washington to a flagship production like ‘Imperfect Women’ underscores Apple TV+'s commitment to an ambitious content expansion, featuring high-stakes sci-fi, dark comedies, and returning hit dramas throughout 2026.

#### The Premiere of ‘Imperfect Women’

The highly anticipated 8-episode psychological thriller starring Kerry Washington will debut on Apple TV+ on March 18. This dramatic series aims to immerse viewers into the complex dynamics among its three main characters as their shared history fractures under immense pressure.

#### The Return of Science Fiction Hits

One of the earliest returns in the 2026 schedule is ‘Monarch: Legacy of Monsters’ Season 2, returning with its 10-episode second season on February 27. The acclaimed space drama 'For All Mankind' also returns for its fifth season on March 27.

Season 5 of ‘For All Mankind’ will leap forward to the 2010s. This season's narrative will pivot to the burgeoning political schism between Earth and Mars, charting the transformation of Martian inhabitants from explorers into a distinct, independent population.

#### New Star-Powered Dark Comedies

Further amplifying the star power is the new series ‘Margo’s Got Money Troubles,’ set to launch on April 15. The series is based on the 2024 novel by Rufi Thorpe and centers on Margo Millet, a financially struggling college dropout and new mother who turns to OnlyFans.

The cast for ‘Margo’s Got Money Troubles’ boasts an exceptionally strong lineup, featuring Elle Fanning, Michelle Pfeiffer, Nick Offerman, and Nicole Kidman in key roles. The eight-episode first season will debut with three episodes on April 15, followed by a weekly release schedule.

### Significance & What's Next

Kerry Washington's role in ‘Imperfect Women’ marks a significant continuation of her work in prestige television, building on her iconic status established in previous dramatic and political series.

The series’ focus on profound psychological themes and complex female relationships aligns perfectly with the layered and powerful characterizations that have defined much of Washington’s career, promising a compelling watch for critics and fans alike.

The 2026 content slate, anchored by Washington's new project, ensures a robust year for the streamer.

Other major returning crime thrillers include ‘Criminal Record’ Season 2, returning on Wednesday, April 22. In this eight-episode season, Peter Capaldi and Cush Jumbo reprise their roles as their murder investigation escalates into an undercover operation to foil a far-right bomb plot in London.

Furthermore, ‘Your Friends & Neighbors’ Season 2 returns to Apple TV+ on Friday, April 3. This installment continues to follow Andrew "Coop" Cooper, a disgraced hedge fund manager turned thief, whose suburban criminal secrets are jeopardized by the arrival of a new neighbor.

### Conclusion

With ‘Imperfect Women’ spearheading a slate of high-quality, star-driven content, Kerry Washington is poised to make a significant impact on the streaming landscape in 2026. Prepare for its psychological depths and compelling drama when the eight-episode series arrives on Apple TV+ this spring.
