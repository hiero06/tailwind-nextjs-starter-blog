## 2025 Daytime Emmy Awards: Nominations Revealed and The Hollywood Reporter's Nod

The National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences (NATAS) has unveiled the nominees for the 2025 Daytime Emmy Awards, promising an exciting ceremony on October 17th in Pasadena, California. Among the contenders are popular shows like 'General Hospital,' 'The Drew Barrymore Show,' and 'The Kelly Clarkson Show,' with The Hollywood Reporter also receiving a nomination for its 'Off Script With The Hollywood Reporter' series.

### Event/Topic Overview

The 2025 Daytime Emmy Awards nominations span various categories, recognizing excellence in daytime television programming. From drama series to talk shows and culinary programs, the nominees represent the best in the industry. The awards ceremony is set to take place on October 17th in Pasadena, California, celebrating the achievements of individuals and teams who contribute to daytime television.

Several new categories will debut this year, including outstanding culinary cultural series, outstanding emerging talent in a daytime drama series, and outstanding regional content in a daytime genre. The nominees in eight categories were initially revealed on 'Access Hollywood,' 'E! News,' 'ET,' and 'Extra,' showcasing the anticipation surrounding this year's awards.

### Key Highlights & Standouts

#### Outstanding Daytime Drama Series

'Days of our Lives' (Peacock), 'General Hospital' (ABC), and 'The Young and the Restless' (CBS) are all nominated in this prestigious category.

#### Outstanding Daytime Talk Series

The nominees include 'The Drew Barrymore Show' (CBS Media Ventures), 'The Jennifer Hudson Show' (Warner Brothers Television Distribution), 'The Kelly Clarkson Show' (NBCUniversal Syndication Studios), 'Live With Kelly and Mark' (Disney Entertainment Distribution), and 'The View' (ABC).

#### Outstanding Entertainment News Series

'Access Hollywood' (NBCUniversal Syndication Studios), 'E! News' (E! Entertainment), 'Entertainment Tonight' (CBS Media Ventures), and 'Extra' (Warner Brothers Television Distribution) are all vying for the award.

#### The Hollywood Reporter's Nomination

 _The Hollywood Reporter_ has received a nomination for its _Off Script With The Hollywood Reporter_ series in the arts and popular culture program category. This recognition highlights the publication's commitment to covering arts and entertainment with insightful and engaging content.
